Don’t Move, Improve! showcases London’s best new home extension projects, as featured in New London Architecture’s annual competition, celebrating projects for demonstrating cost-effective, innovative and creative living spaces for Londoners.

A free exhibition of all Don’t Move, Improve! projects is on show at NLA in WC1 from 27/01—28/04. You can also book for free advice sessions on extending your home with some of these award-winning architects.
